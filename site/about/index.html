About Us

We here at the Duck Cult enforce strict isolation to immerse our members completely into their obsession with ducks. It is used to intensify the focus of their beliefs - in comparison to the ideologies of the Cult, the outside world is irrelevant, as it does not help us to delve into further understanding and enlightmenment.

For more information, read our mission statement.

We use ducks as an all-encompassing aspect of our identites, leaving little room for independent thinking. This allows the collective to achieve a greater harmony together, and to embrace the collective understanding.

For more information, visit our community page.

Our money doesn't grow on trees! We rely on the generous and substantial financial support of our patrons to fund our extravagant rituals, altars, statues and much more. These allow for our members to experience spiritual elevation through financial sacrifice.

For more information, take a look at our sponsors page.

Our Cult does, in our devotion to ducks, allow followers to experience greater enlightenment down the road of physical and emotional suffering, as a necessary sacrifice to appease the higher powers.

The Cult Leader wields absolute authority, allowing for collaboration, harmony, and progress. They are our source of translation from the higher powers, and are the only person one should go to for answers about Ducks, so as to dam the spread of misinformation.
